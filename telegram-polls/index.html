FAQ

Frequently asked questions about Telegram polls

Can I create a poll directly inside Telegram?

Yes. Participants open Telegram WebApp and complete the poll inside one clear interface.

What question types are supported?

You can use single choice, multiple choice, text answer, rating, NPS and contact fields.

Can I check subscription before participation?

Yes. You can add required channels, and TgDraw checks the conditions before the poll starts.

Can I collect contacts through a poll?

Yes. A survey can include name, phone, email and other fields.

Can I export poll results?

Yes. When export is available for your access mode, you can save answers, participants and question statistics.

Is it suitable for channels and groups?

Yes. Polls are suitable for Telegram channels, groups, brands, shops and educational projects.
